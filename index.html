Any Questions? Answered

Lets make sure you get all your questions answered. If you cant find what you are searching for feel free to contact us: support@heste.dev

CSS stands for Comparison Shopping Services. In Europe, the Google Shopping ads on the main search results page have been displayed as price comparison ads for some time. Why? Google relaunched the Google Shopping ad platform under the name Google Shopping Europe (GSE) as a price comparison after the European Commission fined Google a hefty sum in 2017 for discriminating against competing price comparison sites. The restructuring of the entire platform to price comparison created equal opportunities among the participants.
After you register we will add your Google merchant center account to our MCA. We pull all your products that you are actively promoting and add them to our CSS. You don't have to do anything after that, but you can always monitor your performance. You will see your click cost will reduce to up to 20% and your impression share will grow.
As an advertiser, you need to know: With the introduction of Google Shopping Europe (GSE), the composition of your bids for Google Shopping Ads has changed, which are included in the auction for the ad rank. In order to be able to prove to the EU that GSE is profitable as a company, Google takes ~ 20% of your bids as margin. If you advertise your products via another CSS like the heste. One CSS, this margin does not apply. Your bids are included in the auction in full. Your advantage: With a lower bid, you outbid an advertiser who still places his ads via GSE.
To further increase the ad presence, we take advantage of the so-called "Double Listing". This option is available if you continue to run your existing merchant center via Google Shopping or other CSS in addition to your merchant center with us as a CSS partner. This way, both Merchant Centers can play your ads at the same time. However, to avoid your ads competing with each other, a user will never be shown the same ad twice in a search.
Of course. We are working hard on new software and features like feed optimisation, text ads automation etc. For more information checkout our future release plans.
The one time setup usually takes around 10 minutes. All you have to do is login with your merchant center account, enter your merchant center id and off you go. You won’t notice any changes – You can continue advertising as you have had so far, or use the services of third parties or agencies from home and abroad. Continue using your current Google Ads and Google Merchant account as usual. We will do the rest.
